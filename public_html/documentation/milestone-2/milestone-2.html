Content Strategy

Welcome: I will have this next to my profile photo on my header. "Hello, my name is Rae. I am a Front-End Web Developer from
Moriarty, New Mexico"

About Me: This will be a paragraph or two about where I am from, what I do and, what I am able to do professionally.

Portfolio: I will have 3 images of projects I have completed and additional talents and skills that compliment my work
as a Web Developer. I will include brief sentences explaining each project.

Contact Me: This will have my contact form, a thank you message, links to my Linkedin, github, and Pro Dev sites,
and a two line sentence that includes my marketing pitch for myself.
