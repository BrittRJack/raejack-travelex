Purpose of this site

I am creating this site geared towards women who have a desire to travel the world safely and independently. As a solo female traveller myself,
I understand the importance of having a strong community around me to feel that support.
As people of the world we all have that desire to experience new places, cultures and, food. We all deserve to feel safe and secure in our endeavors.

Audience

Single women who would like to travel but are letting fears and circumstances hold them back.

Goal

The main goal is to unite single women who want to travel solo through this site by sharing my stories and experiences
of the do's and don't's and allow travel-seekers to ask questions and receive advice.
