Purpose of this site

I am creating this site geared towards women who have a desire to travel the world safely and independently.

Audience

Single women who would like to travel but are worried about safety and cultural differences.

Goal

The main goal is to unite single women who want to travel solo through this site by sharing my stories and experiences
and allow travel-seekers to ask questions and receive feedback.
