Matanuska Glacier, AK

Summer of 2017 I went to Alaska to work at Sheep Mountain Lodge.
The experience was incredible. The wildlife is abundant and the people are very independent.
Matanuska Glacier is 5 minutes away from Sheep Mountain Lodge. There are tours where you can trek and ice climb the glacier.

Poipu Kauai, Hawaii

Poipu is a beach located in Kauai, HW. This beach is known for its massive sea turtles and
seals. They will often come up on the beach and lay out in the sun. The animal patrol units will set up caution tape around them to make
sure they are not disturbed by the bustling tourists trying to get a closer look. I definitely recommend this place
